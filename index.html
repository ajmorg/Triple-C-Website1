About Us

At Triple C, your satisfaction is our top priority. We will work endlessly to ensure your landscaping needs are met in a quality and timely manner, while costing less than the other guy. Our goal is to make you happy, and we hope you give us the chance to prove it.

Triple C Landscaping was founded in 2015 by best friends Andrew, Charlie, Chase, and Craig, who attend the University of Michigan and Michigan State University. They are proud Grand Blanc and Powers Catholic Alums who enjoy coaching and playing sports, as well as spending time in the sun (perfect for landscaping). But most of all, they love making customers happy and hope to meet you soon!
